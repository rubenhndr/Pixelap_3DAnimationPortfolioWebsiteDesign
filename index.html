About Me

01

Hello! I'm Ruben Handira.

I am a UI/UX Designer and also a Game Developer using Unity Engine who has a great passion for creating unique and meaningful works. With a lot of experience, I have created an Augmented Reality application for animal recognition on Android using Unity.

I believe that good design/content is not only about aesthetics, but also how it conveys a message, builds emotion, and provides an impactful experience for users. Therefore, I always strive to combine creativity with the right strategy in every piece I create.

Some of the things I am good at include designing UI/UX, designing the environment of a game, editing videos by adding 3d elements, and also I like to experiment with different styles and techniques to produce something fresh and innovative.

When I'm not working, I usually spend time playing games and also studying. I'm always open to collaborating on interesting projects, so don't hesitate to contact me!

My Projects

02

Kido AR

This app is an Augmented Reality app created using the Unity Engine.

This application was created as an educational tool for toddlers and children to get to know the world of current technological developments.

This Augmented Reality uses the Marker Based type, where this marker is an animal image from a smart toddler book that can be purchased online as an object to bring 3D animal objects into the real world.

This Kido AR can run on various platforms (depending on needs) such as Android, IOS, and PC.
